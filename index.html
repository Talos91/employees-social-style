Social Style?

Everyone communicates a little differently. This quick check maps how you come across on two dimensions — how much you tell vs. ask, and how much you show vs. control emotion — and places you in one of four styles. There are no better or worse styles; knowing yours just helps us work together better.

  • Analytical — precise, logical, deliberate
  • Driving — decisive, direct, results-first
  • Amiable — warm, supportive, easy-going
  • Expressive — energetic, social, big-picture
